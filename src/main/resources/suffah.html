Suffah Islamic Research Center

Suffah Islamic Research Center is a prestigious bona fide online research center, offering a wide range of services aimed at creating religious awareness among the people and reforming society at large. Renowned Mufti Anas Abdur Raheem (damat barakatuhum), is the founder and CEO of Suffah Islamic Research Center.

Aims and Objectives

  • Dissipating religious knowledge beyond sectarian bias.
  • Reforming society and beliefs, and countering atheism, agnosticism and so-called enlightenment.
  • Countering Qadiyanism, creating awareness amongst the people regarding this trial.
  • Spreading religious knowledge among the people through online courses conducted on social media.
  • Educating Aalimaat in depth about issues particular to women so that women can inquire unhesitatingly about delicate matters.
