Zainab Institute

Zainab Institute is a scholarly and religious institution which provides religious services under the patronage of Hadrat Maulana Muhammad Ejaz Mustafa (damat barakatuhum) Amir of Aalami Majlis Tahaffuz Khatme Nubuwwat, Karachi.

Goals and Objectives

  • Promote and teach Aalimaat the Fiqh of menstruation and nifas.
  • Create a strong team of expert Aalimaat in these injunctions.
  • Draw common women's attention towards the general fiqh of menstruation.
  • Make a warm, welcoming, easy and non-judgemental platform for common women so that they do not shy away from asking about masail-e-haidh and sensitive issues.
  • Take all possible steps for the betterment of the society.
